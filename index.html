WHO WE ARE AND WHY

Lovin Summer is a joint initiative by Tourism Ireland, the Department of Foreign Affairs and the Department of Health with the aim of promoting healthy lifestiles during the summer in Ireland by providing inspiration and ideas for locals and visitors to explore all that our country has to offer.

Outdoor Activities

Immerse yourself in Ireland's stunning landscapes and diverse outdoor activities. From epic hiking along the Cliffs of Moher to exciting water sports on the coast of the Dingle Peninsula, there's something for every adventure lover. Explore ancient castles, delve into the country's history at museums, and relish in the mouthwatering tastes of authentic Irish cuisine.

Why go outside and move?

Discover the transformative power of nature and embrace the joy of outdoor activities.
Engaging in outdoor activities like hiking, biking can improve and boost your physical health.
It helps reduce stress and anxiety.
Outdoor activities provide an opportunity to connect with the environment and develop a deeper sense of environmental consciousness.
Outdoor activities offer a great opportunity to bond with friends, family, or meet like-minded individuals who share your passion for nature and outdoor adventures.
